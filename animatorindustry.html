Animator Industry

In Latin 'Anima' means soul. Animation is all about giving soul to a character.
It is about moving something which cannot move itself.

Some of the popular animation technique are:

  • 2D Cel Animation Also known as traditional animation, 2D animation involves the creation of a high volume of separate drawing that defines a sequence.
  • 3D CGI Animation This technique makes extensive use of animation software programmes, 3D objects are constructed using curves or 2D geometric figures.
  • 3D Motion Capture AnimationThis process of creating 3D characters is similar to the 3D CGI animation technique, however, the technique differ with respect to the time when the animation effects are introduced.
